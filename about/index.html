About Our PTO

Supporting Moore through fundraising, service, and community.

Moore Elementary PTO exists to support students, teachers, and families by funding school needs, strengthening school connections, and helping create meaningful experiences throughout the year.

Who We Are

We are parents, guardians, teachers, and staff partners working together to support Moore Elementary and help every child feel encouraged, connected, and celebrated.

The Moore Elementary PTO exists to serve our school by providing learning and enrichment opportunities for students, supporting teachers and staff, and helping fund the needs of the school through community-driven fundraising.

Everything we do is powered by volunteers. Whether a family can help with one event, contribute behind the scenes, or stay involved throughout the year, every effort helps strengthen the Moore community.

All parents and guardians of Moore Elementary students are automatically members of the PTO, and every family is welcome to get involved in whatever way fits their schedule.

How the PTO Gives Back

Our work reaches students, teachers, and families in many ways throughout the school year. Here are some of the programs, events, and efforts the PTO helps support.

Fill the Nest

Our direct donation campaign helps fund academic needs, school support, and enrichment opportunities that benefit Moore students and staff.

Moore Miles

A school-wide fundraiser that brings together school spirit, movement, and community while raising support for Moore’s needs.

Teacher & Staff Appreciation

Celebrating the teachers and staff who care for Moore students through appreciation events and meaningful support throughout the year.

Book Fair

Promoting reading while helping provide additional resources and books that support the school library and student learning.

Hospitality

Helping create welcoming experiences for staff and families through events such as welcome-back gatherings, appreciation weeks, and community celebrations.

NOOK

Supporting children in need by helping provide clothing, hygiene products, and other necessities to school-aged children across the community.

Room Parents

Giving parents opportunities to stay active in their child’s classroom and support teachers, students, and classroom celebrations.

Spirit Nights

Building relationships with local businesses that give back to Moore while creating fun ways for families to gather and support the school.
