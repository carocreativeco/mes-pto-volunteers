Who We Are

A community of parents, guardians, teachers, and staff.

Working together to help every child feel encouraged, connected, and celebrated.

The Moore Elementary PTO exists to serve our school by providing learning and enrichment opportunities for students, supporting teachers and staff, and helping fund school needs through community-driven fundraising.

Everything we do is powered by volunteers. Whether a family can help with one event, contribute behind the scenes, or stay involved throughout the year, every effort helps strengthen the Moore community. All parents and guardians of Moore Elementary students are automatically members of the PTO. Every family is welcome.

There is no required commitment. We would love to see you involved in whatever way fits your schedule.

Programs & Initiatives

How the PTO gives back.

Our work reaches students, teachers, and families in many ways throughout the school year. These are a few of the programs, events, and efforts the PTO helps support.

Moore Miles

A school-wide fundraiser that brings together school spirit, movement, and community while raising support for Moore’s needs.

Teacher & Staff Appreciation

Celebrating the teachers and staff who care for Moore students through appreciation events and meaningful support throughout the year.

Book Fair

Promoting reading while helping provide resources and books that support the school library and student learning.

Hospitality

Helping create welcoming experiences for staff and families through welcome-back gatherings, appreciation weeks, and community celebrations.

Room Parents

Giving parents opportunities to stay active in their child’s classroom and support teachers, students, and classroom celebrations.

Spirit Nights

Building relationships with local businesses that give back to Moore while creating fun ways for families to gather and support the school.
