About Our PTO

Bringing families, teachers, and staff together.

The Moore Elementary PTO supports students and staff by organizing events, funding school needs, and helping create a welcoming, connected school community.

Everything we do is powered by volunteers and community support. From Moore Miles and Book Fair to teacher appreciation and classroom celebrations, every contribution helps make a real difference. All parents and guardians of Moore Elementary students are automatically members of the PTO.

Whether you can help with one event, contribute behind the scenes, or stay involved throughout the year — there’s a place for every Moore family. Your time directly supports students and strengthens the school and the Franklin community.

How We Help

Funding needs, supporting teachers, and building community at Moore.

We support Moore through fundraising, teacher appreciation, volunteer efforts, and community events that make school more connected, welcoming, and fun.
