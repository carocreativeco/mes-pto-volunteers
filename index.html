About Our PTO

We bring families, teachers, and staff together to support Moore Elementary through fundraising, volunteer efforts, and community-building throughout the school year.

The Moore Elementary PTO supports students and staff by organizing events, funding school needs, and helping create a welcoming, connected school community.

Everything we do is powered by volunteers and community support. From Moore Miles and Book Fair to teacher appreciation and classroom celebrations, every contribution helps make a real difference.

All parents and guardians of Moore Elementary students are automatically members of the PTO, and every family is welcome to get involved.
