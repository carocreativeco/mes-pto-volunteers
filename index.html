About Our PTO

The Moore Elementary PTO is a volunteer organization of parents, guardians, and teachers working together to support our students and school. We organize events, fundraisers, and programs that enrich the educational experience for every child at Moore.

Our PTO runs entirely on the energy of our amazing volunteers. Whether you can give an hour or a whole day, every bit of help makes a difference. From Moore Miles to Teacher Appreciation Week, Book Fairs to classroom parties — there's a place for everyone to contribute!

All parents and guardians of Moore Elementary students are automatically members of the PTO. We encourage everyone to get involved in whatever way works for your schedule.
