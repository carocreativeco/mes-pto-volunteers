About Our PTO

We bring families, teachers, and staff together to support Moore Elementary through events, volunteer efforts, and community-building throughout the school year.

The Moore Elementary PTO organizes events, fundraisers, and programs that enhance the educational experience for every child at Moore. Our work helps build a welcoming, connected community for students, families, and staff.

Everything we do is powered by volunteers. Whether you can give an hour here and there or help more regularly throughout the year, every contribution makes a real difference. From Moore Miles to Teacher Appreciation Week, Book Fairs to classroom celebrations, there are many ways to participate.

All parents and guardians of Moore Elementary students are automatically members of the PTO. We encourage everyone to get involved in whatever way fits their schedule.
